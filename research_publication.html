Current UNESCO Project

As UNESCO Chair on AI-Supported Digital Platforms in Education and Healthcare at LUT University and Professor Emeritus at the University of Jyväskylä, Pekka Neittaanmäki aligns the project initiative “AI-Supported Digital Platforms in Parental Education and Mental Healthcare.”

In his scholarly capacity as Chairholder, he supports the research dimensions of this project. Specifically endorsing its potential to:

  • Advance comparative research linking Malaysia and Finland on reflective cognition, digital ecosystems, and parental well-being;
  • Generate evidence-based insights to inform national policy and international discussions on AI in education and healthcare;
  • Contribute to UNESCO’s mission of inclusive, human-centered digital transformation.

He encourages academic partners, policymakers, and community stakeholders to collaborate with Dr. Elaine on developing the research aspects of this initiative — with implementation led by local partners in Malaysia to contribute valuable knowledge to the global dialogue on parental well-being and digital innovation.
