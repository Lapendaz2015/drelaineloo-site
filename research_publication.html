UNESCO Chair Endorsement

Prof. Pekka Neittaanmäki

UNESCO Chair on AI-Supported Digital Platforms in Education & Healthcare, LUT University · Professor Emeritus, University of Jyväskylä

“Pekka Neittaanmäki aligns and endorses our initiative AI-Supported Digital Platforms in Parental Education and Mental Healthcare, highlighting its capacity to advance inclusive, human-centered innovation.”

Key impacts he champions
  • Advance comparative research linking Malaysia and Finland on reflective cognition, digital ecosystems, and parental wellbeing.
  • Generate evidence-based insights that inform national policy and international discussions on AI in education and healthcare.
  • Contribute to UNESCO’s mission of inclusive, human-centered digital transformation.

Prof. Neittaanmäki invites academic partners, policymakers, and community leaders to collaborate with Dr. Elaine in developing the research dimensions, while Malaysian partners lead implementation that feeds valuable knowledge back into the global dialogue on parental wellbeing and digital innovation.
