About Me

A Journey Rooted in Diversity

Growing up in Malaysia meant navigating cultural differences, neurodivergence, and the many “streams” of education. These experiences shaped Dr. Elaine Loo’s conviction: education must go beyond grades and excel collectively. Out-think in a world where AI can outthink humans in IQ, what matters most is nurturing the fullness of human potential.

The Passion: Why 5Q Matters

Elaine excelled in academics, proving the strength of IQ. Yet she came to a profound realization: if machines can outcalculate us, what remains uniquely human?

The answer lies in EQ (emotional regulation), AQ (adaptability), SQ (social meaning‑making), and UQ (identity awareness). Her 5Q MindVehicle™ Framework unites these with IQ, not as competition, but as collaboration. When people live with 5Q, families connect more deeply, educators inspire with meaning, and communities rise together.
